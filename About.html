System FAQ

Is NeoQuiz free? +

Yes. NeoQuiz is an open-access platform developed to support education in Arba Minch. No payment is required.

How do I calculate my score? +

The system calculates it automatically! You get 1 point for every correct answer. Your percentage is shown immediately after the quiz.

Are these questions from previous national exams? +

Yes. The Grade 12 and University Entrance questions are compiled from previous Ethiopian National Exams and Freshman course sheets.
